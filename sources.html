Sources & Attribution

Documentation of primary sources used in this historical chart archive

Primary Source: FAA Aeronautical Charts

Publisher Information

The aeronautical sectional charts displayed on this website are produced and published by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), an agency of the United States Department of Transportation.

United States. Federal Aviation Administration. VFR Sectional Aeronautical Charts. Washington, D.C.: Federal Aviation Administration, [various dates 2005-2025]. Digital images. Scale 1:500,000.

Publication Details

  • Publisher: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)
  • Publication Frequency: Updated every 56 days
  • Scale: 1:500,000
  • Coverage: Conterminous United States, Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories
  • Date Range: 2005 - Present

Original Distribution

FAA aeronautical charts are published as official U.S. government documents and distributed through the FAA's Aeronautical Information Services. As U.S. government works, these charts are in the public domain and not subject to copyright protection under 17 U.S.C. § 105.

United States. Federal Aviation Administration. Aeronautical Information Services. https://www.faa.gov/air_traffic/flight_info/aeronav/

Archival Source: Internet Archive

Historical chart editions not currently available from the FAA were retrieved from the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine, a digital archive of the World Wide Web.

Internet Archive. Wayback Machine. San Francisco, CA: Internet Archive, 1996-. https://web.archive.org/ Accessed [various dates 2024-2025].

Archive Details

  • Organization: Internet Archive (501(c)(3) non-profit)
  • Archive Type: Web archive snapshots
  • Original URLs Archived: FAA Digital Products distribution pages

The underlying base map uses data from OpenStreetMap and CARTO.

OpenStreetMap contributors. OpenStreetMap. OpenStreetMap Foundation, 2004-. https://www.openstreetmap.org/ Licensed under the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL).

Technical Implementation

Libraries Used

  • Leaflet: Open-source JavaScript library for interactive maps. https://leafletjs.com/
  • PMTiles: Single-file tile archive format. https://github.com/protomaps/PMTiles
  • GDAL: Geospatial Data Abstraction Library for raster processing. https://gdal.org/

Data Processing

Original chart images were georeferenced, reprojected to Web Mercator (EPSG:3857), and converted to PMTiles format for efficient web delivery. No alterations were made to the chart content itself.
