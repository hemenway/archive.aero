Where the charts and map data on archive.aero come from.

⚠️ Historical use only

Every chart shown on archive.aero is a historical scan and is not current. Do not use these charts for navigation. Always consult official, in-date FAA publications and aeronautical information for flight planning and operations.

Historical aeronautical charts

The historical Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Sectional Aeronautical Charts displayed here are digitized scans held by the U.S. National Archives and Records Administration (NARA), within Record Group 237 — Records of the Federal Aviation Administration. The original charts were produced by the FAA (and its predecessor agencies) and are works of the U.S. federal government.

Chart scans are processed into Cloud-Optimized GeoTIFFs and served as PMTiles / WebP raster tiles. archive.aero is an independent project and is not affiliated with, or endorsed by, the FAA or NARA.
