✈️ Timeline gap & coverage prototypes

All variants are driven by real availability: coverage.json, precomputed from dates.csv + the geographic bounds in each of the 3,201 PMTiles headers. Drag any timeline to scrub. The big blank stretch is the real 1972–2010 gap; current dates show as full coverage even though they're a single merged archive.

A — Coverage heat strip (replaces the plain track)

The track itself becomes an availability map driven by real spatial coverage (union of every archive's PMTiles bounds vs the lower 48): teal = full US coverage, amber = partial (brighter = more area), hatched red = nothing in the database. Sparse decades are visibly dimmer while you scrub.
