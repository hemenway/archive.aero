How you can help

Old sectionals turn up in hangars, flight bags, estate sales, and library basements all the time — most people don't realize anyone wants them. If any of the charts below are hiding in your attic, they can fill a hole in the historical record for an entire region.

📜 Have old charts?

Any pre-2000 sectional is worth a look, especially from the eras listed below. Even a phone photo of the legend panel is enough to identify the edition — email one over and we'll figure out the best way to get it scanned.

🔎 Know a collection?

A museum, university library, flying club, or private collector with a drawer of old charts — or an online scan we've missed. A pointer to where charts live is just as valuable as the charts themselves.

☕ Chip in

The site is free and stays that way. Donations go toward hosting and toward scanning and shipping costs when charts are found.
